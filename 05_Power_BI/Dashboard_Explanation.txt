Dashboard Explanation

The Power BI dashboard was developed to provide an interactive view of the Indian Electric Vehicle (EV) market. It allows users to analyze EV sales, 
manufacturer performance, state-wise trends and market penetration through different dashboard pages.

Home Page

The home page provides a brief introduction to the project and acts as the navigation page. Users can directly access the Executive Overview, Maker Analysis
and State Analysis dashboards.

Executive Overview

This page presents a high-level summary of the Indian EV market using key performance indicators such as Total EV Sales, Total Vehicles Sold, 
EV Penetration (%) and Total Makers. It also highlights sales trends over fiscal years, vehicle category performance, and state-wise EV sales to provide 
an overall market overview.

Maker Analysis

This dashboard focuses on the performance of EV manufacturers in India. It compares market share, total EV sales, yearly sales trends and manufacturer 
rankings across different fiscal years. The analysis helps identify leading manufacturers and understand the competitive landscape of the Indian EV market.

State Analysis

This page analyzes EV adoption across different states in India. It highlights state-wise EV sales, EV penetration, geographical distribution and product preferences for different vehicle categories. The dashboard also summarizes the key findings to identify high-performing markets and future growth 
opportunities.

Dashboard Features

- Interactive slicers for Fiscal Year, State, Maker, and Vehicle Category.
- Cross-filtering across visuals for better analysis.
- KPI cards to highlight important business metrics.
- Multiple charts and maps for comparing market performance.
- Business-focused design to support data-driven decision-making.
