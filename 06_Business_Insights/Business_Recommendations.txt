Business Recommendations

Based on the findings from this analysis, the following recommendations can support AtliQ Motors in planning its expansion into the Indian EV market.

- Prioritize market entry in high-performing states such as Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu, where EV demand is already well established.
- Consider Kerala as a strategic market because of its high EV penetration and strong consumer acceptance of electric vehicles.
- Focus initially on the 2-Wheeler segment, as it represents the largest share of the current EV market.
- Study the strategies of leading manufacturers such as Ola Electric to better understand pricing, product positioning, and market competition.
- Expand gradually into states with lower EV penetration but high overall vehicle sales, as these markets offer long-term growth opportunities.
- Strengthen partnerships with charging infrastructure providers to improve customer confidence and support EV adoption.
- Monitor yearly sales trends and regional performance regularly to identify changes in market demand and customer preferences.
- Use data-driven insights to support product launch planning, regional marketing strategies, and future business expansion decisions.

These recommendations are based on the historical sales data analyzed in this project and are intended to support informed business planning rather than predict future market performance.
