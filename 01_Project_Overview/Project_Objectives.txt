Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

- Study the current Electric Vehicle (EV) market in India.
- Analyze EV sales trends across different fiscal years.
- Compare the performance of states based on EV sales and market penetration.
- Identify the leading EV manufacturers in the Indian market.
- Compare the performance of different vehicle categories.
- Perform data cleaning, validation, and exploratory data analysis (EDA).
- Answer business questions using SQL queries.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business reporting.
- Generate meaningful insights and business recommendations to support market expansion.
